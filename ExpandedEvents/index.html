Official opening of the Minority Educator Recruitment, Retention & Equity Center (MERREC)

Tuesday, Nov 12, 2019 • 3:30pm to 5:30pm
Oliver Hall - Education - 4064B, Monroe Park Campus

For VCU School of Education family, students and community partners:

You are all cordially invited to the opening and ribbon cutting ceremony for the Minority Educator Recruitment, Retention & Equity Center (MERREC) on Tuesday, November 12th from 3:30 - 5:30 pm.

MERREC is a grant funded center housed in the School of Education, Room 4064b, that will serve as a safe hub for underrepresented minority (URM) educators across programs, and supporting URM educators in our local school divisions. Specifically, MERREC will serve as a space where (a) culturally responsive mentoring and induction, (b) culturally responsive professional development, and (c) research efforts will take place to ultimately help with understanding how to better recruit, prepare, and retain URM educators. Additionally, financial assistance, to serve the recruitment, retention, and professional needs of URM educators are available through the center.

So please stop by and join us for some light refreshments on November 12th as we celebrate the opening of this ground-breaking space. Please also forward this message to any students or community partners that you think may be interested in learning more about MERREC.
